Food of the Valley Blog Site

About the Project

Food of the Valley is a blog all about local food and drinks here in Southern Oregon. The topics we cover include food, restaurants, wine, and coffee. WordPress was our platform of choice for this project and we used Twitter and Instagram for promotion.

WordPress Blog Site

WordPress makes a great platform for a project like this because of its ease of use and feature set. This platform also makes it very easy to track analytics and over it's just a great experience for both the viewer and the author.​​​​​​​
